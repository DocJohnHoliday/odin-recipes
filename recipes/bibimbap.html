Bibimbap

Description

Bibimbap is a traditional Korean dish. Meant to be served as the main dish with kimchi, seaweed, and other small sides.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Rice
  • Carrots
  • Seaweed
  • Bulgogi
  • Gochujang

Steps

  1. Make the rice
  2. Cook the meat
  3. Add the ingredients
  4. Enjoy!
